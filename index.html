My City

Akluj has been named after goddess "Aklai Devi". The temple of "Aklai devi" is on the banks of river Nira. Akluj boasts of number of co-operative institutes, educational institutions, historical destinations and amusement spots. Therefor it has been included in the approved list of rural tourist spots of Government of Maharashtra. Temples of Aklai devi, Anandi Ganesh and Parvati devi help the tourists to connect themselves to the spiritual side of the life. The Ground Fort and the Shisrushti in it adds to the historical significance of the place and the institutions such as sugar factory, Shivamrut milk dairy and a spinning mill provide an opportunity to the educational tourists.

Tourists Places Akluj

Anandi Ganesh Temple:

The Exquisitely crafted anandi Ganesh temple in anandnangar Locality of Akluj is one of famous tourists places in Akluj.It was decided to build the temple of lord ganesh on the anandi ganesh hill.during excavation,a shivling is idol of god mahadev, was found on the site.considering it as a good sign ,the beautiful shivalaya -with the facility of meditation and the library,is also built on the hill.at the foot of the anandi ganesh hill, there is a spcious hall (anadi ganesh karyalaya )with all requirements amenities,to organise various function.

Godess Aklai Temple:

Late Smt. Ratrnaprabhadevi Mohite-Patil alias Akkasaheb, along with a committee, renovated this long ignored temple of Goddess Aklai devi. The entire area of the temple was renovated gradually under the guidance of Hon. Shri. Vijaysinh Mohite-Patil and now the temple in its modern and attractive decor surrounded by the garden attracts the tourists. It has become one of the prime tourist destinations in Akluj.

Parvati Temple

Sahkar Maharshi Shankarrao Mohite-Patil tried to combine spirituality with modernity and there for he renovated the old temple of lord shiva in its present form of the idol of Lord Shiva with Uma together. It is now known as parvati mandir. It is indeed a delightful place.the grand entrance gate welcomes the visitors to have a look at an imposing statue of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj with a beautifully landscaped garden featuring a japanese pagoda, the temple and the marvelous natural view. Every year to mark the occasion of Mahashivratri, an agro based exhibition,crop competitions,wrestling competitions and some other informative and recreational events are organized near the temple to boost the local farmers to achieve excellence in their field.
